The Four Seasons

Symbolism | Travel | Images
About
The four seasons, spring, summer, autumn, winter, are 4 different distinct periods of a time that happen once a year. Each season has its own temperature range, light, and weather patterns. However, each season is not experinced by every area of the world. Depending on where you live and your proximity to the equator, your country might only experince 1, 2, 3 or all 4 of the seasons.
Symbolism of the Seasons
Season Meaning
Spring rebirth, rejuvenation, renewal, resurrection, regrowth
Summer happiness, hope, joy
Autumn harvest, abundance, decay, decline
Winter self reflection, sadness, silence
